Weapons & Traps

Here are the weapons and traps available in Fortress Simulator.

Weapons

Cannons

Make big explosions that damage all zombies in the area.

Wall Cannons

Can be placed as blocks and used as functional weapons simultaneously.

Player Cannon

A controllable weapon that functions like a bazooka.

Ultra Mortar

A controllable weapon similar to a tank turret, dealing high explosive damage.

Flamethrower

Sprays zombies with fire.

Ice Turrets

Sprays zombies with ice.

Repair Turrets

Automatically shoot and heal blocks within range.

Bazookas

The fastest-firing explosive weapon.

Tank Turrets

Deal heavy damage but are not the most powerful weapon.

Super Turrets

An upgraded version of Tank Turrets, dealing even more damage.

Plasma Cannons

Fires faster than a Bazooka, deals as much damage as a Super Turret, and is the most powerful weapon.

Traps

Spikes

Deal damage to zombies when touched.

Launch Pads

Throw zombies into the air, instantly killing them.

Sticky Glue

Slows down zombies, making them easier to hit.
