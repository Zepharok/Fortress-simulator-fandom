Blocks

This page will contain information about the different blocks in Fortress Simulator.

Available Blocks

  • Brick Block: A sturdy block made of bricks.
  • Armored Block: A reinforced block designed for durability.
  • Stone Block: A solid block made from stone.
  • Metal Block: A strong block crafted from metal.
  • Wood Block: A basic block made of wood.
  • Wood Stairs: Stairs made from wood for easy access.
  • Stone Stairs: Durable stairs made from stone.
  • Metal Stairs: Strong stairs crafted from metal.
  • Armored Stairs: Reinforced stairs for extra protection.
  • Armored Wedge: A wedge-shaped block for stabilization.
  • Stone Wedge: A stone wedge for versatile building.
  • Brick Wedge: A brick wedge for creative designs.
  • Metal Wedge: A metal wedge for enhanced support.
  • Wood Wedge: A wooden wedge for flexible construction.
